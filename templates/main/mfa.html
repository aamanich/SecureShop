Multi-Factor Authentication Setup

Add an extra layer of security to your account

Why use Multi-Factor Authentication?

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) adds an additional layer of security to your account. Even if someone discovers your password, they won't be able to access your account without the second factor.

SecureShop requires MFA for high-value transactions and account changes to protect your data and payment information.

Choose your verification method

Use an app like Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or Authy to generate verification codes.

Recommended

Receive a verification code via text message to your registered phone number.

  1. Download an authenticator app like Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or Authy.
